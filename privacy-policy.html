Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2, 2025

At Favish Inc, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information obtained through our Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) application, Favish Analytics.

Information We Collect

Our application may access the following data through Amazon SP-API, depending on the roles granted:

  • Amazon catalog and inventory data
  • Buy Box and pricing insights
  • Advertising performance metrics
  • Sales performance and historical order volume
  • Store or account-level identifiers

We do not collect or store personally identifiable customer data unless explicitly required and approved under Amazon's restricted data access program.

How We Use Your Data

We use SP-API data to:

  • Analyze and visualize business performance
  • Recommend ad spend optimization strategies
  • Benchmark product performance
  • Support internal reporting and planning

We never sell, rent, or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256 or equivalent)
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only, with role-based controls
  • We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the requested functionality or as required by law

Third Parties

We do not share SP-API data with third parties, except as needed to:

  • Provide hosting or infrastructure (e.g., Google Cloud)
  • Comply with legal obligations

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

Your Rights

You can revoke your authorization to our app at any time through your Amazon Seller Central account.

Contact

For any questions about data privacy, contact us at: privacy@favish.com
